Download Started!

Your macOS workstation download is in progress. Follow these 3 simple steps to launch LexSort Core:

1

Open the Installer Package

Once the download is complete, click LexSort_Core_v1.0.0.dmg in your browser's download bar or locate it in your Downloads folder and double-click to mount the drive.

2

In the window that opens, drag the LexSort Core app icon (🛰️) directly into the Applications shortcut folder (📂) to install the program natively.

3

Launch & Open Onboarding

Go to your Mac's Applications folder, double-click lexsort-core, and click 'Open' on the standard Gatekeeper confirmation prompt to start your private setup wizard!
