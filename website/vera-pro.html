Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Pro subscription and licensing flow.

How do I receive my license key?

Immediately after Stripe checkout completes, your license key will be displayed directly on the success screen. Copy and paste this key into VERA to activate Pro features. Stripe will send a receipt to your email address (required for payment processing), but VERA itself never stores, requests, or uses your email address.

Does the license key contact the internet?

No. When you paste your key, VERA parses it, verifies the signature locally against its hardcoded public key, checks the expiration date, and binds the license to your hardware fingerprint. No network calls are made during activation or use.

Can I use Pro on multiple computers?

No. A single active subscription is for one (1) desktop installation. However, when we launch the LexSort-GO module, you will be allowed to link one (1) mobile device to your setup.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

When your subscription ends, your license key will expire and VERA will revert to Freeware mode, meaning you will retain full access to VERA Chat, but Pro modules (Emailer, Guardian, Mobile pair) will become locked until renewal.
